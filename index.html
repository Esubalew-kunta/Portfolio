About Me

Hi, I’m Esubalew Kunta, a Software Engineer and Applied AI enthusiast with hands-on experience in building cross-platform applications and intelligent systems. My work focuses on combining structured data, scalable platforms, and evaluation frameworks to address real-world challenges.

During my studies, I designed and implemented projects such as Balemuya, a geolocation-based job matching system, and an Amharic language text classifier, both aimed at solving practical problems in Ethiopia. These experiences helped me understand the importance of reliable systems, data integrity, and adaptable models under constrained environments.

I am passionate about advancing my skills in applied AI, particularly in creating systems that are interpretable, robust, and beneficial for communities. My goal is to translate technical knowledge into impactful solutions that improve access to services and opportunities, while continuing to learn from innovative research in multilingual and data-scarce contexts.
